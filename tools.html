How These Tools Work

1RM Estimator

Uses the Epley formula (Weight × (1 + Reps/30)) to estimate your one-rep max. Most accurate for sets of 1–6 reps. Use it to track strength progress and set your training percentages without risky maximal attempts.

Volume Guide

Research-backed recommendations for weekly working sets per muscle group. Based on the MEV/MAV/MRV framework. Beginners need less volume to grow; advanced lifters need significantly more to continue adapting.

Intensity Calculator

Converts your 1RM into percentages for structured training zones. Use 65–75% for technique and hypertrophy, 80–85% for strength, 85–95% for power and competition peaking. Rounds to nearest 2.5 lbs for real-world loading.

TDEE Calculator

Uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate, then multiplies by your activity factor to get Total Daily Energy Expenditure. Cut at −500 cal/day to lose ~1 lb/week. Lean bulk at +300 cal/day to gain muscle with minimal fat.
